About Us

Creating positive change across Nepal since 2010

Nepal Green Alliance is a grassroots organization founded by local environmental activists and social workers committed to addressing Nepal's most pressing environmental and social challenges.

Through community engagement, education, and sustainable development projects, we're building a healthier, more resilient Nepal for future generations.

Our Missions

Four pillars of positive change

Reducing Pollution

Leading initiatives to combat air and water pollution in Kathmandu Valley through community clean-ups, education, and sustainable technology adoption.

Promoting Afforestation

Restoring Nepal's forests through community-led planting initiatives, conservation efforts, and sustainable forestry training programs.

Building Infrastructure

Constructing and maintaining schools and healthcare facilities in underserved rural communities to improve education and healthcare access.

Disaster Relief

Providing immediate assistance and long-term recovery support to communities affected by earthquakes, floods, landslides, and other natural disasters.
